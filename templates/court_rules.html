Basic Court Rules

  • All parties must arrive at the court on time.
  • Electronic devices should be switched off or silenced inside the courtroom.
  • Respect the judge, court staff, and legal professionals at all times.
  • Only speak when permitted by the judge or court personnel.
  • Present all necessary documents in an organized manner.
  • Dress appropriately in formal or respectful attire.
  • Maintain silence during court proceedings unless addressed.
  • Do not record or photograph court proceedings without permission.
